At Frisco Custom Pools, we understand that a clean pool is a happy pool. Our comprehensive cleaning services include skimming the surface, vacuuming the pool floor, brushing walls and tiles, and maintaining proper chemical balances. We use state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly products to ensure your pool water is crystal clear, healthy, and inviting.

Repair Services:
Our repair services cover a wide range of needs, from fixing leaks and replacing worn-out parts to addressing more complex issues like pump and filter repairs. We pride ourselves on our quick response times and efficient problem-solving skills, ensuring minimal downtime so you can enjoy your pool without interruption.

Electrical Knowledge and Services:
Frisco Custom Pools also specializes in pool electrical services. Our team is well-versed in the latest electrical standards and safety protocols. Whether it's installing energy-efficient pool lighting, repairing electrical pool heaters, or setting up automated pool systems, we have the skills and knowledge to handle all your pool's electrical needs.
