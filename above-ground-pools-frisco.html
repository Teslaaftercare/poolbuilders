Installing a Funsicle Oasis Above Ground Pool

Transforming your backyard into a summer oasis is easier than you might think with the Funsicle Oasis Above Ground Pool. This 14 ft. Round, 42 in. Deep Metal Frame Pool, with its natural teak print, is not only a refreshing escape from the heat but also a stylish addition to your outdoor space. In this comprehensive guide, we'll walk you through the steps of installing your Funsicle Oasis Pool, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable setup process.

Preparation: Choosing the Right Location

Before you begin, it's crucial to select an appropriate location for your pool. The area should be firm, flat, and level. Avoid sandy or loose ground and ensure the spot is free of stones, sharp objects, or any debris that could damage the pool. If your pool includes a filtration system, position it 10 to 21 feet away from an electrical outlet for safety and convenience.

Step 1: Unpacking and Organizing

Upon receiving your Funsicle Oasis Pool, which retails for $585.96 at Home Depot, unpack all components and lay them out for easy access. Your package includes the pool liner with a natural teak print, durable oval poles, a SureStep ladder, a SkimmerPlus Filter pump, and a ground cloth for additional protection.

Step 2: Assembling the Pool

If you've purchased a ground cloth, lay it down first. Then, unfold the pool liner, allowing it to warm in the sun for about two hours to make it more pliable. Stretch the liner as flat as possible. Assemble the frame by connecting the horizontal beams and T fittings, ensuring all pins snap securely into place. Slide the vertical legs through the liner belt and attach them to the T fittings, ensuring they're perpendicular to the ground for stability.

Step 3: Adding Water

Begin filling the pool with one inch of water, then pause to smooth out any wrinkles on the pool floor. This step is crucial for ensuring a smooth and comfortable base.

Step 4: Setting Up the Filtration System

For those using the SFX filtration system, locate the inlet and outlet holes on your pool. Insert the suction and return fittings as per the instructions. Attach the hose to the skimmer and return fitting, ensuring the hose clamps are tightened over the O-rings. Continue filling the pool, adjusting as necessary to ensure even filling.

Step 5: Final Touches

Once the pool reaches about six inches below the top rim, balance the pH and chlorine levels using a pool test strip. If using the SFX system, insert the rest of the canister parts and set up the GFCI power cord. The pump power is controlled by a daily timer, which can be configured as per the user guide.

Conclusion

Installing your Funsicle Oasis Above Ground Pool is a straightforward process that promises endless fun and relaxation. With its elegant design and durable construction, this pool is a fantastic investment for families, party enthusiasts, and anyone looking to enjoy the pleasures of swimming in the comfort of their backyard. Remember to review any HOA rules before purchasing and always prioritize safety during installation and use. Happy swimming!
